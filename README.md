# mc-piano-masonry
A repo to store the music scores of piano tutorials uploaded to the YT channel <a href="https://www.youtube.com/@MC-Piano-Masonry">@MC - Piano Masonry</a> (formerly known as MC - Music on Piano). 

# How to locate the scores?

Just use the search function, accessible by clicking 'T' on your keyboard (on PC). 

# What scores are included?

Please see list.md

# Why are you uploading them for free?

Philantropy i guess

But freebies come at a cost. You only get to download the heavily watermarked and low image quality PDF for free. If you want the 'clean' version, as well as the MIDI and MP3 files, please go to <a href="https://mcpianomasonry.gumroad.com/">my Gumroad page</a>.
